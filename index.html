Welcome to Nigerians In Diaspora Organisation Thailand

Nigerians in Diaspora Organisation (NIDO) is geared towards homeland development. This Organisation unites Thailand based Nigerians to collectively deploy the skills and talents of Nigerians for nation building. Every Nigerian is a professional in their own remit, be it Education, Vocational fields, Health, Information Technology, Businesses and or Entrepreneurship and much more. Join us to work together towards this worthy goal.

See Our Projects

Mission & Vision

The central vision of NIDO Thailand chapter is to bring Nigerians living in Thailand together and to identify those willing to offer their skills (Education, Information Technology, Health, Economic, science, Administration, Law, Political and Corporate Governance, Management etc.) to assist Nigeria's national development.

These skills will be needed in developmental projects by government and non-governmental organisations. NIDO Thailand chapter as a result requires those Nigerians living in the Thailand who can afford to spend a period of time in Nigeria to contribute their skills to help Nigeriaâ€™s developmental process. NIDO Thailand chapter provides the unique opportunity for Nigerians living in Thailand to learn to work and collaborate with one another in the task of nation building of our homeland.

Aims & Objectives

NIDO Thailand is a non-profit organization with no affiliation to any political party. It is established to pursue the following: -

  1. To maintain a database of Nigerian professionals of all disciplines in Thailand.
  2. To promote Nigeria as a destination of investment in Africa.
  3. To liaise with all levels of government in Nigeria on the socio-economic and technological development of Nigeria.
  4. To liaise with all levels of government in Thailand on matters of interest to Nigeria and Nigerians in Thailand.
  5. To source for prospective Thai entrepreneurs to invest in Nigeria.
